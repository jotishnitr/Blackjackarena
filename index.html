Blackjack

Goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Game Rules

  • Enter your name, age (18+), and starting chips
  • Before each round, enter your bet amount
  • Click START GAME to begin a new round
  • Your bet is deducted at the start of the round
  • You will receive two cards initially
  • Each card adds to your total sum
  • Ace counts as 11 or 1 automatically to prevent bust
  • Click NEW CARD to draw another card
  • If your sum exceeds 21, you lose your bet
  • If your sum becomes exactly 21, you win and get double your bet
  • Finish the current round before starting a new one
  • The game ends when you run out of chips
